About

AFKERIAN INTERACTIVE

AFKERIAN INTERACTIVE is an independent studio building mobile games and utility applications. The current portfolio spans genres — from the grid-strategy combat of XO Arcade: Infinity Edition, to the neon local-multiplayer hockey of Air Strike Arcade, to the rhythm and world exploration of Tap Odyssey — alongside QR Decoder, an offline scanning and document utility with on-device OCR.

Every title in this portfolio is designed around the same engineering discipline: keep core functionality local to the device, minimize what is collected, and disclose plainly what third-party services are used for advertising, analytics, and crash reporting. This portal exists to make that documentation easy to find.

Offline-First

Core gameplay and utility features are designed to run without a live cloud dependency.

On-Device Processing

Document scanning and OCR in QR Decoder run locally via Google ML Kit, not on remote servers.

Data Minimization

No account registration is required, and we do not directly collect names or addresses.

Transparent Compliance

Every title publishes its Privacy Policy, Terms, and third-party notices on this portal.
