Studio

Who we are.

AFKERIAN INTERACTIVE is an independent studio building mobile games and utility applications. Our portfolio spans genres — grid-strategy combat, local-multiplayer arcade, and rhythm exploration — alongside QR Decoder, an offline document and scanning utility with on-device OCR.

Every title shares the same engineering discipline: keep core functionality local to the device, minimize what's collected, and disclose plainly which third-party services support advertising, analytics, and crash reporting.

Principles

How we build.

01

Creativity

Grid-strategy combat, local-multiplayer arcade, and rhythm exploration — one studio, deliberately different systems rather than one formula repeated four times.

02

Local-First Engineering

Core gameplay and utility features are designed to run on-device — including document OCR in QR Decoder, powered locally by Google ML Kit rather than a remote server.

03

Privacy

No account registration is required, and we do not directly collect names or addresses. What third-party SDKs process is disclosed plainly in every Privacy Policy.

04

Performance

No network round-trip stands between you and core functionality — scanning, gameplay, and progression run locally, without waiting on a live cloud dependency.

05

Transparency

Every title publishes its Privacy Policy, Terms, and third-party notices in the open — reachable from this site in at most two clicks.
