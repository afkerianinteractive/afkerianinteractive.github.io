Data / privacy note
Google ML Kit APIs may process input data such as images, video frames, or text on-device and may not send that input data or resultant outputs to Google servers, except as otherwise described by Google’s applicable terms, documentation, or service-specific behavior.

Google ML Kit APIs may contact Google servers from time to time to receive items such as bug fixes, updated models, and hardware accelerator compatibility information. Google ML Kit APIs may also send metrics about the performance and utilization of the APIs in the App to Google.

ML Kit Android SDKs may collect device information, application information, device or other identifiers, performance metrics, diagnostics, and usage analytics as described in Google’s ML Kit Android data disclosure guidance. Google states that the data listed in that guidance is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and is not transferred to third parties.

For the Google Play services ML Kit Document Scanner component, models, scanning logic, UI flow, and related resources may be delivered through Google Play services. The document scanner flow is user-directed, and Google documentation states that users retain control over the scanning process and can send digitized files back to the App.

The App’s data disclosures and user choices are described in the App’s Privacy Policy.
