Verified implementation statement
Firebase Analytics automatic default SDK collection is enabled and is not gated by UMP. Firebase Crashlytics is enabled only in release builds, is disabled in debug builds, and is not gated by UMP.

The App does not require Studio account registration for Firebase Analytics. No application-authored Firebase Analytics events, Studio-controlled user IDs, or Studio-created user properties were identified in the verified implementation.

No application-authored custom keys, custom logs, free-text user IDs, non-fatal reports, test-crash UI data, or user-content reporting were identified in the verified Crashlytics implementation.

Data / privacy note
Firebase Analytics may collect and process app interaction events, app lifecycle events, app-instance identifiers, Advertising ID where available/configured, masked IP-derived coarse location, device/technical information, and related analytics signals.

When enabled in a release build, Firebase Crashlytics may collect and process crash and ANR logs, stack traces, relevant application state, device metadata, app state, Crashlytics installation identifiers, Firebase installation identifiers, native symbols, and related diagnostic information when crashes or stability events occur.

Firebase data is processed by Google/Firebase under applicable Google and Firebase terms and policies. The Studio may view related analytics, crash reporting, and diagnostic information in Firebase Console.

The Studio does not use Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics to sell personal information. The Studio does not share Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics data with non-Google third parties for advertising.

Firebase is separate from Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK. Advertising-related collection, processing, and sharing by Google AdMob is described separately in the App’s Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK Notice and in the App’s Privacy Policy.
