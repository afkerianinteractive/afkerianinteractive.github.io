GOOGLE ADMOB — THIRD-PARTY SERVICE NOTICE

The App uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) provided by Google LLC to display advertising. The verified implementation remains pending production AdMob identifiers, published UMP messages, regional runtime QA, Google Play Data Safety reconciliation, and qualified legal review; this notice does not state production readiness or legal approval.

In the verified implementation, the App uses an anchored adaptive banner only on the Home screen, occasional interstitials only on eligible module-to-Home exits, and an optional rewarded ad that removes banners and interstitials for exactly 60 minutes. The App does not implement App Open ads or native ads. Ads are not shown while scanning, editing, saving, or exporting. Ad failures do not block navigation or destroy work. The 60-minute benefit does not disable UMP, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, the voluntary rewarded entry point, or all network activity.
